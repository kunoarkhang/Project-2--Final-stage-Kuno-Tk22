Why Newmagazine?

"Virgil Abloh, Sandy Liang, and Kim Jones, each recognized as innovators in the fashion world, came together in 2022 driven by a shared vision. They wanted to create a fashion news magazine website that would break barriers, redefine industry norms, and foster inclusivity. Their motivation stemmed from a desire to provide a platform that celebrates diverse styles, promotes emerging talent, and delivers captivating content to fashion enthusiasts worldwide. By combining their unique perspectives and expertise, they aimed to create a digital hub that showcases the ever-evolving landscape of fashion, pushing boundaries and inspiring a new generation of creatives."
